Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Weightwatch - Body Weight Log is an offline-first app. All entries you log are stored only in the app's local storage on your device (Application Support directory). We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We have no servers, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. We never see your payment details.

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. There is no account, no sign-in, and no data to request or delete from us because none is ever collected.
